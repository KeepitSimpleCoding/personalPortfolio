About Me

Hi, I’m Ryan.

I am a documentation and quality-focused professional with over 15 years of experience in analytical writing, structured reporting, and regulated government environments. I have held a Top Secret clearance and collaborated with federal agencies, statewide government entities, and military branches where precision, discretion, and reliability were essential.

Today, I focus on data quality and technical documentation, combining investigative discipline with systems thinking. I work with SQL and Python and continue to strengthen my technical skills while building projects that blend documentation, quality assurance, and thoughtful problem solving.

Outside of work, I am a dedicated father of four. My children are my greatest motivation and the reason I care deeply about building stable, meaningful work. I value clarity, discipline, continuous learning, and creating systems that improve both efficiency and understanding.
